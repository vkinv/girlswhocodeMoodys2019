Our Ambition

People all over the world are severely impacted by food waste, yet this is an issue that lacks proper attention. 1.3 billion tons of food a year is wasted, enough to feed 2 billion people. That’s a third of all the food grown worldwide! Hundreds of millions of people around the world suffer from hunger, lacking enough food to live a healthy life, and greenhouse gas emissions are increasing, playing a large role in accelerating climate change. If we are able to decrease the amount of wasted food, rates of hunger as well as greenhouse gas emissions would significantly decrease.

At Better Food for a Better World, our ambition is simple. We strive to educate and inform people about food waste to bring awareness to the growing issue, sparking change.
